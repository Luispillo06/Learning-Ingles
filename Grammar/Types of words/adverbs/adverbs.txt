================================================================================
                              ADVERB FORMATION
================================================================================

Most adverbs of manner are formed by adding -LY to adjectives:

ADJECTIVE           ADVERB
---------           ------
slow                slowly
quick               quickly
careful             carefully
beautiful           beautifully
easy                easily (y → ily)
terrible            terribly (le → ly)
full                fully (ll → lly)
true                truly (e → ly)

EXCEPTIONS (same form for adjective and adverb):
- fast → fast
- hard → hard
- late → late
- early → early
- daily → daily

IRREGULAR:
- good → well
- bad → badly

================================================================================
                              ADVERB POSITION
================================================================================

1. FREQUENCY ADVERBS: Before main verb, after "be"
   → She always arrives on time.
   → He is never late.

2. MANNER ADVERBS: Usually at the end
   → She speaks English fluently.

3. TIME ADVERBS: Usually at the beginning or end
   → Tomorrow I'll call you. / I'll call you tomorrow.

4. PLACE ADVERBS: Usually at the end
   → I looked everywhere.

5. DEGREE ADVERBS: Before the word they modify
   → She is very intelligent.
