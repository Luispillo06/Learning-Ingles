================================================================================
                           PREPOSITIONS IN ENGLISH
                          (Preposiciones en Inglés)
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                        PREPOSITIONS OF PLACE (Lugar)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

IN          - en, dentro de         → The book is in the box.
ON          - sobre, encima de      → The cup is on the table.
AT          - en (lugar específico) → I'm at the station.
UNDER       - debajo de             → The cat is under the bed.
OVER        - sobre, por encima     → The plane flew over the city.
ABOVE       - encima de, arriba     → The picture is above the sofa.
BELOW       - debajo de, abajo      → The temperature is below zero.
BETWEEN     - entre (dos cosas)     → The bank is between the store and the café.
AMONG       - entre (varios)        → She was among friends.
BEHIND      - detrás de             → The garden is behind the house.
IN FRONT OF - delante de            → The car is in front of the building.
NEXT TO     - al lado de            → The pharmacy is next to the hospital.
BESIDE      - junto a               → Come sit beside me.
NEAR        - cerca de              → The school is near the park.
BY          - junto a, cerca de     → The lamp is by the window.
INSIDE      - dentro de             → The keys are inside the drawer.
OUTSIDE     - fuera de              → Wait outside the door.
OPPOSITE    - enfrente de           → The bank is opposite the supermarket.
AROUND      - alrededor de          → There are trees around the lake.
THROUGH     - a través de           → Walk through the tunnel.
ACROSS      - al otro lado de       → The store is across the street.
ALONG       - a lo largo de         → Walk along the river.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                        PREPOSITIONS OF TIME (Tiempo)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

IN          - en (meses, años, siglos, partes del día)
              → in January, in 2024, in the morning

ON          - en (días, fechas específicas)
              → on Monday, on December 25th, on my birthday

AT          - a (horas, momentos específicos)
              → at 5 o'clock, at noon, at midnight, at night

BEFORE      - antes de              → Before lunch, I go for a walk.
AFTER       - después de            → After work, I go home.
DURING      - durante               → During the movie, don't talk.
FOR         - durante, por          → I studied for two hours.
SINCE       - desde                 → I've lived here since 2010.
UNTIL/TILL  - hasta                 → Wait until tomorrow.
BY          - para (fecha límite)   → Finish it by Friday.
FROM...TO   - desde...hasta         → From 9 to 5.
WITHIN      - dentro de             → I'll call you within an hour.
PAST        - pasado (horas)        → It's half past three.
AGO         - hace                  → Two days ago.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                      PREPOSITIONS OF MOVEMENT (Movimiento)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

TO          - a, hacia              → Go to the store.
FROM        - de, desde             → I come from Spain.
INTO        - hacia dentro          → She walked into the room.
OUT OF      - fuera de              → He ran out of the building.
ONTO        - sobre (movimiento)    → The cat jumped onto the table.
OFF         - fuera de (superficie) → Take your feet off the table.
UP          - arriba, hacia arriba  → Climb up the stairs.
DOWN        - abajo, hacia abajo    → Go down the hill.
THROUGH     - a través de           → Drive through the tunnel.
ACROSS      - a través de           → Swim across the river.
ALONG       - a lo largo de         → Walk along the beach.
AROUND      - alrededor de          → Run around the track.
TOWARDS     - hacia                 → He walked towards the door.
PAST        - por delante de        → Walk past the church.
OVER        - por encima de         → Jump over the fence.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                       PREPOSITIONS OF MANNER (Modo)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BY          - por, mediante         → Travel by car. Pay by credit card.
WITH        - con                   → Write with a pen. Go with me.
WITHOUT     - sin                   → Coffee without sugar.
LIKE        - como                  → She sings like an angel.
AS          - como (función)        → He works as a teacher.
IN          - en (idioma, manera)   → Say it in English. Write in ink.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                       PREPOSITIONS OF PURPOSE (Propósito)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FOR         - para                  → This gift is for you.
TO          - para (infinitivo)     → I came to help.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                       PREPOSITIONS OF CAUSE (Causa)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BECAUSE OF  - debido a, por         → Because of the rain, we stayed home.
DUE TO      - debido a              → The delay was due to traffic.
OWING TO    - debido a              → Owing to illness, she missed class.
THANKS TO   - gracias a             → Thanks to you, we succeeded.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                       OTHER COMMON PREPOSITIONS (Otras)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ABOUT       - sobre, acerca de      → Tell me about your day.
OF          - de (pertenencia)      → The color of the sky.
WITH        - con                   → Coffee with milk.
WITHOUT     - sin                   → A day without rain.
AGAINST     - contra                → Lean against the wall.
DESPITE     - a pesar de            → Despite the rain, we went out.
EXCEPT      - excepto               → Everyone came except John.
INSTEAD OF  - en lugar de           → Tea instead of coffee.
ACCORDING TO - según                → According to the news...
APART FROM  - aparte de             → Apart from that, everything is fine.
AS FOR      - en cuanto a           → As for me, I prefer tea.
BEYOND      - más allá de           → Beyond the mountains.
CONCERNING  - con respecto a        → Concerning your question...
REGARDING   - con respecto a        → Regarding your email...
