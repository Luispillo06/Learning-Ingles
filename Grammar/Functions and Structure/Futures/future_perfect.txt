================================================================================
                          FUTURE PERFECT
                   (Futuro Perfecto en Inglés)
================================================================================

El Future Perfect describe acciones que estarán COMPLETADAS antes de
un momento específico en el futuro.

================================================================================
                         STRUCTURE (Estructura)
================================================================================

AFFIRMATIVE (Afirmativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SUBJECT    +    WILL HAVE    +    PAST PARTICIPLE (V3)                     │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  I/You/He/She/It/We/They    will have    finished/arrived/eaten             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ I will have finished by 5 o'clock.
→ She will have arrived by then.
→ They will have eaten before we get there.
→ By next year, I'll have graduated.

CONTRACTION: will have = 'll have
→ I'll have finished.
→ She'll have left.

NEGATIVE (Negativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SUBJECT    +    WON'T HAVE    +    PAST PARTICIPLE (V3)                    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  I/You/He/She/It/We/They    won't have    finished/arrived                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ I won't have finished by 5.
→ She won't have arrived yet.
→ They won't have completed the project.

INTERROGATIVE (Interrogativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  WILL    +    SUBJECT    +    HAVE    +    PAST PARTICIPLE    +    ?        │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Will         I/you/he/she/it/we/they    have    finished?                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ Will you have finished by 5?
→ Will she have arrived by then?
→ Will they have completed the project?

SHORT ANSWERS:
→ Will you have finished?    Yes, I will. / No, I won't.

================================================================================
                         USES (Usos)
================================================================================

1. COMPLETED ACTION BEFORE A FUTURE TIME (Acción completada antes de):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ I will have finished the report by 5 pm.                                      │
│ Habré terminado el informe para las 5.                                        │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By the time you arrive, I will have left.                                     │
│ Para cuando llegues, ya me habré ido.                                         │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She will have graduated by next summer.                                       │
│ Ella se habrá graduado para el próximo verano.                                │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By the end of the year, we will have saved $10,000.                           │
│ Para fin de año, habremos ahorrado $10,000.                                   │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ The movie will have started by the time we get there.                         │
│ La película habrá empezado para cuando lleguemos.                             │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

2. DURATION UNTIL A FUTURE POINT (Duración hasta un punto futuro):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ By next month, I will have lived here for 10 years.                           │
│ El mes que viene, habré vivido aquí 10 años.                                  │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By December, she will have worked here for 5 years.                           │
│ En diciembre, ella habrá trabajado aquí 5 años.                               │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By the time he's 30, he will have been a doctor for 5 years.                  │
│ Para cuando tenga 30, habrá sido médico durante 5 años.                       │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Next week, they will have been married for 25 years.                          │
│ La semana que viene, llevarán 25 años casados.                                │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

3. ACHIEVEMENTS BY A CERTAIN TIME (Logros para cierto momento):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ By the end of this course, you will have learned 1000 words.                  │
│ Al final de este curso, habrás aprendido 1000 palabras.                       │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By 2030, scientists will have found a cure.                                   │
│ Para 2030, los científicos habrán encontrado una cura.                        │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By next year, I will have read 50 books.                                      │
│ El año que viene, habré leído 50 libros.                                      │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    KEY WORDS AND TIME EXPRESSIONS
================================================================================

BY + TIME/DATE (para, antes de):
    by 5 o'clock           → para las 5
    by tomorrow            → para mañana
    by next week           → para la semana que viene
    by the end of the year → para fin de año
    by 2030                → para 2030
    by then                → para entonces

BY THE TIME + PRESENT (para cuando):
    by the time you arrive      → para cuando llegues
    by the time she comes       → para cuando venga
    by the time we finish       → para cuando terminemos

BEFORE (antes de):
    before you get here         → antes de que llegues
    before the meeting          → antes de la reunión

IN + TIME PERIOD:
    in two hours               → en dos horas
    in a week                  → en una semana
    in a year                  → en un año

================================================================================
                    FUTURE PERFECT vs FUTURE SIMPLE
================================================================================

┌──────────────────────────────────────┬────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ FUTURE SIMPLE                        │ FUTURE PERFECT                         │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Action in the future                 │ Action COMPLETED before future time   │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ I will finish at 5.                  │ I will have finished by 5.             │
│ (Terminaré a las 5)                  │ (Habré terminado para las 5)           │
│ = terminando a las 5                 │ = completado ANTES de las 5            │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She will arrive at 8.                │ She will have arrived by 8.            │
│ (Llegará a las 8)                    │ (Habrá llegado para las 8)             │
│ = a las 8 llega                      │ = en algún momento antes de las 8      │
└──────────────────────────────────────┴────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    BY vs UNTIL
================================================================================

BY = completed before this point (completado antes de):
→ I will have finished BY 5 pm. (completado en algún momento antes de las 5)

UNTIL = continuing up to this point (continúa hasta):
→ I will work UNTIL 5 pm. (trabajaré hasta las 5)

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ BY 5 pm, I will have finished. (action complete before 5)                     │
│ I will work UNTIL 5 pm. (working continues to 5)                              │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                           COMMON MISTAKES (Errores comunes)
================================================================================

  ❌ WRONG                              ✓ CORRECT
  ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  I will have finish by 5.              I will have finished by 5.
  She will has arrived.                 She will have arrived.
  By 5, I will finished.                By 5, I will have finished.
  I will have went home.                I will have gone home.
  Until 5, I will have finished.        By 5, I will have finished.
  By the time you arrive, I left.       By the time you arrive, I will have left.
  I will have ate dinner.               I will have eaten dinner.
