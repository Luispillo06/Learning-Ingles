================================================================================
                    PRESENT CONTINUOUS FOR FUTURE
                  (Presente Continuo para el Futuro)
================================================================================

El Present Continuous puede usarse para hablar del FUTURO cuando nos 
referimos a planes y citas fijos, especialmente con hora y lugar definidos.

================================================================================
                         STRUCTURE (Estructura)
================================================================================

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SUBJECT    +    BE (am/is/are)    +    VERB-ING    +    FUTURE TIME        │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  I                  am                   meeting          him tomorrow.     │
│  He/She/It          is                   leaving          at 5 pm.          │
│  You/We/They        are                  having           dinner tonight.   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ I'm meeting John at 3 o'clock.
→ She's flying to Paris tomorrow.
→ We're having a party on Saturday.
→ They're getting married next month.

================================================================================
                         USES (Usos)
================================================================================

1. FIXED ARRANGEMENTS (Planes fijos con fecha/hora):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ I'm meeting Sarah at 7 pm.                                                    │
│ Me reúno con Sarah a las 7. (está en mi agenda)                               │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She's seeing the doctor on Monday.                                            │
│ Ella tiene cita con el médico el lunes.                                       │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ We're having dinner with the Smiths tonight.                                  │
│ Cenamos con los Smith esta noche.                                             │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ They're arriving at 10 am.                                                    │
│ Llegan a las 10 de la mañana.                                                 │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

2. TRAVEL AND TRANSPORT (Viajes y transporte):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ I'm flying to London next week.                                               │
│ Vuelo a Londres la semana que viene.                                          │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She's leaving tomorrow morning.                                               │
│ Ella sale mañana por la mañana.                                               │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ We're taking the train at 8:15.                                               │
│ Tomamos el tren a las 8:15.                                                   │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ He's driving to Madrid on Friday.                                             │
│ Él conduce a Madrid el viernes.                                               │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

3. SOCIAL EVENTS (Eventos sociales):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ We're getting married in June.                                                │
│ Nos casamos en junio.                                                         │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ They're having a baby.                                                        │
│ Están esperando un bebé. / Van a tener un bebé.                               │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ I'm starting a new job next Monday.                                           │
│ Empiezo un nuevo trabajo el próximo lunes.                                    │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She's throwing a party this weekend.                                          │
│ Ella da una fiesta este fin de semana.                                        │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
           PRESENT CONTINUOUS vs BE GOING TO vs WILL
================================================================================

┌────────────────────┬───────────────────────────┬──────────────────────────────┐
│ PRESENT CONTINUOUS │ BE GOING TO               │ WILL                         │
├────────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────────┤
│ FIXED ARRANGEMENT  │ INTENTION/PLAN            │ SPONTANEOUS DECISION         │
│ (confirmed)        │ (decided but maybe not    │ (decided now)                │
│                    │ arranged)                 │                              │
├────────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────────┤
│ I'm meeting Tom    │ I'm going to meet Tom     │ I'll meet Tom sometime.      │
│ at 3 pm.           │ soon.                     │ (decide now)                 │
│ (it's in my diary) │ (I intend to, but when?)  │                              │
├────────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────────┤
│ We're flying to    │ We're going to travel     │ We'll probably visit         │
│ Paris tomorrow.    │ to Europe.                │ Europe someday.              │
│ (tickets bought)   │ (intention)               │ (prediction/possibility)     │
├────────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────────┤
│ She's seeing the   │ She's going to see a      │ She'll see a doctor if it    │
│ doctor at 2.       │ doctor.                   │ gets worse.                  │
│ (appointment made) │ (intention)               │ (conditional)                │
└────────────────────┴───────────────────────────┴──────────────────────────────┘

NIVEL DE CERTEZA (más seguro → menos seguro):

Present Continuous  >  Be Going To  >  Will
(plan confirmado)      (intención)     (predicción/decisión)

================================================================================
                    COMMON VERBS WITH FUTURE MEANING
================================================================================

Verbos que se usan frecuentemente con Present Continuous para el futuro:

MEETING/SEEING:
→ I'm meeting John tomorrow.
→ She's seeing the dentist next week.

TRAVEL:
→ I'm flying to Rome on Monday.
→ We're leaving early tomorrow.
→ They're arriving at 6 pm.
→ He's coming home next week.

GOING:
→ I'm going to a concert tonight.
→ She's going to the gym later.

HAVING/DOING:
→ We're having a party on Saturday.
→ They're doing a presentation tomorrow.

STARTING/FINISHING:
→ I'm starting my new job on Monday.
→ She's finishing university in June.

================================================================================
                    PRESENT SIMPLE FOR FUTURE
================================================================================

El PRESENT SIMPLE también puede usarse para el futuro con:

HORARIOS Y PROGRAMAS (timetables, schedules):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ The train leaves at 8:15.                                                     │
│ El tren sale a las 8:15. (horario fijo)                                       │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ The movie starts at 7 pm.                                                     │
│ La película empieza a las 7 pm.                                               │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ The shop opens at 9 am.                                                       │
│ La tienda abre a las 9.                                                       │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ What time does the flight arrive?                                             │
│ ¿A qué hora llega el vuelo?                                                   │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ School finishes at 3:30.                                                      │
│ El colegio termina a las 3:30.                                                │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Nota: Present Simple = horarios oficiales que no controlamos
      Present Continuous = nuestros planes personales

COMPARACIÓN:

→ The train leaves at 6. (horario oficial)
→ I'm leaving at 6. (mi plan personal)

→ The movie starts at 8. (programación del cine)
→ I'm starting work at 8. (mi plan)

================================================================================
                           COMMON MISTAKES (Errores comunes)
================================================================================

  ❌ WRONG                              ✓ CORRECT
  ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  I meeting him tomorrow.               I'm meeting him tomorrow.
  She is meet the doctor.               She is meeting the doctor.
  We are go to Paris tomorrow.          We're going to Paris tomorrow.
  What you are doing tonight?           What are you doing tonight?
  I'm meet John at 5.                   I'm meeting John at 5.
  They arriving at 10.                  They're arriving at 10.

================================================================================
                           QUICK SUMMARY
================================================================================

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                      PRESENT CONTINUOUS FOR FUTURE                           │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ STRUCTURE:  Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing + future time expression         │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ USE:        For FIXED ARRANGEMENTS and APPOINTMENTS                          │
│             (Things already organized, in your diary/calendar)               │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ EXAMPLES:                                                                    │
│ • I'm meeting John at 3 pm. (appointment)                                    │
│ • She's flying to Paris tomorrow. (ticket bought)                            │
│ • We're having dinner with friends tonight. (arranged)                       │
│ • They're getting married next month. (date set)                             │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ REMEMBER:                                                                    │
│ • Must have a future time word (tomorrow, tonight, next week...)             │
│ • More definite than "be going to"                                           │
│ • Implies the arrangement is confirmed                                       │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
