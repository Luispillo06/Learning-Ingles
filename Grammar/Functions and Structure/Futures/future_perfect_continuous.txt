================================================================================
                     FUTURE PERFECT CONTINUOUS
               (Futuro Perfecto Continuo en Inglés)
================================================================================

El Future Perfect Continuous enfatiza la DURACIÓN de una actividad
que habrá estado en progreso hasta un momento específico del futuro.

================================================================================
                         STRUCTURE (Estructura)
================================================================================

AFFIRMATIVE (Afirmativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SUBJECT    +    WILL HAVE BEEN    +    VERB-ING                            │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  I/You/He/She/It/We/They    will have been    working/waiting/studying      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ By 5 pm, I will have been working for 8 hours.
→ Next month, she will have been living here for a year.
→ They will have been waiting for two hours by then.
→ I'll have been studying English for 5 years next June.

NEGATIVE (Negativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SUBJECT    +    WON'T HAVE BEEN    +    VERB-ING                           │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  I/You/He/She/It/We/They    won't have been    working/waiting              │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ I won't have been waiting long.
→ She won't have been working here for very long.

INTERROGATIVE (Interrogativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  WILL  +  SUBJECT  +  HAVE BEEN  +  VERB-ING  +  ?                          │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Will      you         have been     working     for long?                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ Will you have been waiting long?
→ How long will she have been working here?

================================================================================
                         USES (Usos)
================================================================================

1. DURATION OF ACTIVITY UP TO A FUTURE POINT (Duración hasta un momento):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ By 6 pm, I will have been working for 10 hours.                               │
│ A las 6, habré estado trabajando durante 10 horas.                            │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By the time she arrives, I will have been waiting for two hours.              │
│ Para cuando llegue, habré estado esperando dos horas.                         │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Next month, we will have been living here for 5 years.                        │
│ El mes que viene, habremos estado viviendo aquí 5 años.                       │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By 2030, they will have been married for 25 years.                            │
│ Para 2030, habrán estado casados 25 años.                                     │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ In June, I will have been studying English for 3 years.                       │
│ En junio, habré estado estudiando inglés durante 3 años.                      │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

2. CAUSE OF A FUTURE SITUATION (Causa de una situación futura):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ She'll be tired because she will have been working all day.                   │
│ Estará cansada porque habrá estado trabajando todo el día.                    │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ He'll be exhausted. He will have been driving for 8 hours.                    │
│ Estará agotado. Habrá estado conduciendo 8 horas.                             │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ My eyes will be tired. I will have been reading all day.                      │
│ Mis ojos estarán cansados. Habré estado leyendo todo el día.                  │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

3. ONGOING ACTIVITY CONTINUING INTO THE FUTURE (Actividad que continúa):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ By next year, I will have been working here for 10 years.                     │
│ El año que viene, llevaré 10 años trabajando aquí.                            │
│ (Y probablemente seguiré trabajando aquí)                                     │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By 2026, she will have been teaching for 20 years.                            │
│ Para 2026, llevará 20 años enseñando.                                         │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    KEY WORDS AND TIME EXPRESSIONS
================================================================================

BY + TIME/DATE:
    by 5 pm                     → para las 5
    by next year                → para el año que viene
    by the end of the month     → para fin de mes

BY THE TIME:
    by the time you arrive      → para cuando llegues
    by the time she finishes    → para cuando termine

FOR + DURATION:
    for 2 hours                 → durante 2 horas
    for 10 years                → durante 10 años
    for a long time             → durante mucho tiempo

HOW LONG:
    How long will you have been waiting?
    ¿Cuánto tiempo habrás estado esperando?

================================================================================
       FUTURE PERFECT vs FUTURE PERFECT CONTINUOUS
================================================================================

┌──────────────────────────────────────┬────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ FUTURE PERFECT                       │ FUTURE PERFECT CONTINUOUS              │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Emphasizes COMPLETION                │ Emphasizes DURATION                    │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By 5, I will have read 3 books.      │ By 5, I will have been reading         │
│ (Habré leído 3 libros - completado)  │ for 4 hours.                           │
│                                      │ (Habré estado leyendo 4 horas)         │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Focus on RESULT/QUANTITY             │ Focus on PROCESS/DURATION              │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She will have finished the book.     │ She will have been reading             │
│ (habrá terminado - resultado)        │ all morning. (habrá estado - proceso)  │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Numbers, quantities                  │ Time expressions (for, since)          │
│ 3 books, 5 reports, 10 pages         │ for 2 hours, all day, since 9 am       │
└──────────────────────────────────────┴────────────────────────────────────────┘

EXAMPLES TO COMPARE:

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ By 6 pm, I will have written 3 reports. (quantity - what I accomplished)     │
│ By 6 pm, I will have been writing for 4 hours. (duration - how long)         │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ By next month, I will have run 100 km. (total distance)                       │
│ By next month, I will have been running for a year. (duration of activity)   │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    STATIVE VERBS
================================================================================

Los STATIVE VERBS normalmente NO se usan en Future Perfect Continuous.
En estos casos, usamos Future Perfect:

know, believe, understand, want, need, like, love, hate,
own, belong, have (possession), be

❌ I will have been knowing her for years.
✓ I will have known her for 10 years by then.

❌ She will have been owning the house for 5 years.
✓ She will have owned the house for 5 years.

================================================================================
                           COMMON MISTAKES (Errores comunes)
================================================================================

  ❌ WRONG                              ✓ CORRECT
  ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  I will have been work for 8 hours.    I will have been working for 8 hours.
  She will been waiting for hours.      She will have been waiting for hours.
  I will have been knowing him.         I will have known him. (stative verb)
  By 5, I will have been waited.        By 5, I will have been waiting.
  How long you will have been working?  How long will you have been working?
  I'll have been study for 3 hours.     I'll have been studying for 3 hours.
