================================================================================
                            PAST CONTINUOUS
                      (Pasado Continuo en Inglés)
================================================================================

El Past Continuous (también llamado Past Progressive) se usa para hablar
de acciones que estaban EN PROGRESO en un momento específico del pasado.

================================================================================
                         STRUCTURE (Estructura)
================================================================================

AFFIRMATIVE (Afirmativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SUBJECT    +    WAS/WERE    +    VERB-ING    +    COMPLEMENT               │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  I/He/She/It       was            working          at 8 pm.                 │
│  You/We/They       were           working          at 8 pm.                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ I was working at 8 o'clock last night.
→ She was sleeping when I called.
→ They were watching TV.
→ He was studying all evening.

NEGATIVE (Negativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SUBJECT    +    WAS/WERE + NOT    +    VERB-ING                            │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  I/He/She/It       wasn't              working.                             │
│  You/We/They       weren't             working.                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ I wasn't sleeping when you called.
→ She wasn't listening to me.
→ They weren't paying attention.
→ He wasn't working yesterday.

INTERROGATIVE (Interrogativo):

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  WAS/WERE    +    SUBJECT    +    VERB-ING    +    ?                        │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Was              I/he/she/it       working?                                │
│  Were             you/we/they       working?                                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

→ Were you sleeping when I called?
→ Was she working yesterday?
→ What were they doing?
→ Where was he going?

SHORT ANSWERS (Respuestas cortas):

→ Were you sleeping?       Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't.
→ Was she working?         Yes, she was. / No, she wasn't.

================================================================================
                         USES (Usos)
================================================================================

1. ACTION IN PROGRESS AT A SPECIFIC TIME (Acción en progreso):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ At 8 o'clock last night, I was watching TV.                                   │
│ A las 8 anoche, estaba viendo la tele.                                        │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ This time yesterday, she was flying to Paris.                                 │
│ A esta hora ayer, ella estaba volando a París.                                │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ What were you doing at midnight?                                              │
│ ¿Qué estabas haciendo a medianoche?                                           │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ At 3 pm, we were having lunch.                                                │
│ A las 3 pm, estábamos almorzando.                                             │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

2. INTERRUPTED ACTIONS (Acciones interrumpidas):
   Past Continuous (long action) + Past Simple (interruption)

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ I was sleeping when the phone rang.                                           │
│ Estaba durmiendo cuando sonó el teléfono.                                     │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She was cooking when the guests arrived.                                      │
│ Ella estaba cocinando cuando llegaron los invitados.                          │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ While I was walking home, I saw an accident.                                  │
│ Mientras caminaba a casa, vi un accidente.                                    │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ He was having a shower when the doorbell rang.                                │
│ Él se estaba duchando cuando sonó el timbre.                                  │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

STRUCTURE:
→ WHEN + Past Simple (short action), Past Continuous (long action)
→ Past Continuous (long action) + WHEN + Past Simple (short action)
→ WHILE + Past Continuous, Past Simple

3. PARALLEL ACTIONS (Acciones simultáneas):
   Two actions happening at the same time

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ While I was cooking, my husband was watching TV.                              │
│ Mientras yo cocinaba, mi esposo veía la tele.                                 │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She was reading while he was sleeping.                                        │
│ Ella leía mientras él dormía.                                                 │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ The children were playing while the parents were talking.                     │
│ Los niños jugaban mientras los padres hablaban.                               │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ I was listening to music while I was working.                                 │
│ Escuchaba música mientras trabajaba.                                          │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

4. BACKGROUND DESCRIPTION (Descripción de fondo):
   Setting the scene in a story

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ The sun was shining. Birds were singing. People were walking in the park.    │
│ El sol brillaba. Los pájaros cantaban. La gente caminaba en el parque.        │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ It was raining heavily. The wind was blowing. No one was on the streets.     │
│ Llovía mucho. El viento soplaba. No había nadie en las calles.                │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Everyone was laughing and having a good time.                                 │
│ Todos reían y la pasaban bien.                                                │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

5. TEMPORARY SITUATIONS IN THE PAST (Situaciones temporales):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ In those days, I was working at a supermarket.                                │
│ En esos días, trabajaba en un supermercado.                                   │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She was living with her parents while she looked for a flat.                  │
│ Ella vivía con sus padres mientras buscaba un piso.                           │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ We were staying at a hotel during the conference.                             │
│ Nos quedábamos en un hotel durante la conferencia.                            │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

6. ANNOYING PAST HABITS with ALWAYS (Hábitos molestos):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ He was always complaining about everything!                                   │
│ ¡Siempre estaba quejándose de todo!                                           │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ She was constantly interrupting me!                                           │
│ ¡Constantemente me interrumpía!                                               │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ They were always arguing.                                                     │
│ Siempre estaban discutiendo.                                                  │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    WHEN vs WHILE
================================================================================

WHEN + Past Simple (short action):
→ I was reading when the phone rang.
→ When she arrived, we were having dinner.

WHILE + Past Continuous (long action):
→ While I was reading, the phone rang.
→ I met him while I was walking to work.

BOTH actions with WHILE (parallel actions):
→ While I was cooking, she was cleaning.
→ While he was studying, I was watching TV.

================================================================================
                    PAST SIMPLE vs PAST CONTINUOUS
================================================================================

┌──────────────────────────────────────┬────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ PAST SIMPLE                          │ PAST CONTINUOUS                        │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Completed action                     │ Action in progress                     │
│ I read a book yesterday.             │ I was reading at 8 pm.                 │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Short action                         │ Long action (interrupted)              │
│ The phone rang.                      │ I was sleeping when...                 │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Sequence of events                   │ Background description                 │
│ I arrived, sat down, and started.    │ The sun was shining. Birds were...     │
├──────────────────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Main events of a story               │ Setting the scene                      │
│ Suddenly, a man appeared.            │ I was walking down the street.         │
└──────────────────────────────────────┴────────────────────────────────────────┘

EXAMPLE STORY:

"I was walking (background) down the street when I saw (main event) an old friend. 
She was carrying (background) a lot of bags. I helped (main event) her and we 
decided (main event) to have coffee. While we were talking (parallel), it started 
(interruption) to rain."

================================================================================
                    TIME EXPRESSIONS (Expresiones de tiempo)
================================================================================

AT + TIME:
    at 8 o'clock, at midnight, at noon

THIS TIME + PAST:
    this time yesterday, this time last week

WHEN:
    when I arrived, when you called

WHILE:
    while I was working, while she was sleeping

ALL + TIME PERIOD:
    all day, all morning, all night, all evening

DURING:
    during the meeting, during the party

================================================================================
                           COMMON MISTAKES (Errores comunes)
================================================================================

  ❌ WRONG                              ✓ CORRECT
  ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  I was work yesterday.                 I was working yesterday.
  She was sleeping when I was calling.  She was sleeping when I called.
  When I was arriving, she left.        When I arrived, she left.
  While I cooked, he watched TV.        While I was cooking, he was watching TV.
  I were working all day.               I was working all day.
  They was playing football.            They were playing football.
  What you were doing?                  What were you doing?
  I was knowing the answer.             I knew the answer. (stative verb)
  She was wanting coffee.               She wanted coffee. (stative verb)
  I was see him yesterday.              I saw him yesterday.
