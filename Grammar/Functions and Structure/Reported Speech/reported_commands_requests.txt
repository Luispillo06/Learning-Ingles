================================================================================
               REPORTED COMMANDS, REQUESTS AND ADVICE
          (Órdenes, Peticiones y Consejos en Estilo Indirecto)
================================================================================

================================================================================
                    BASIC STRUCTURE
================================================================================

Commands, requests, and advice use INFINITIVE structure:

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  VERB + PERSON + (NOT) + TO + INFINITIVE                                    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  He told   me         to     wait.                                          │
│  She asked  him   not  to     leave.                                        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    REPORTED COMMANDS (ORDERS)
================================================================================

Common verbs: TELL, ORDER, COMMAND, INSTRUCT

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "Wait here."                                                        │
│ REPORTED: She told me to wait there.                                          │
│           Me dijo que esperara allí.                                          │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Close the door."                                                   │
│ REPORTED: He told me to close the door.                                       │
│           Me dijo que cerrara la puerta.                                      │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Sit down!"                                                         │
│ REPORTED: The teacher ordered them to sit down.                               │
│           El profesor les ordenó que se sentaran.                             │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Stop talking!"                                                     │
│ REPORTED: She commanded us to stop talking.                                   │
│           Nos ordenó que dejáramos de hablar.                                 │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Follow the instructions carefully."                                │
│ REPORTED: He instructed us to follow the instructions carefully.              │
│           Nos instruyó que siguiéramos las instrucciones con cuidado.         │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

NEGATIVE COMMANDS:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "Don't touch that!"                                                 │
│ REPORTED: She told me not to touch that.                                      │
│           Me dijo que no tocara eso.                                          │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Don't be late!"                                                    │
│ REPORTED: He told us not to be late.                                          │
│           Nos dijo que no llegáramos tarde.                                   │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Never do that again!"                                              │
│ REPORTED: She told him never to do that again.                                │
│           Le dijo que nunca volviera a hacer eso.                             │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    REPORTED REQUESTS
================================================================================

Common verbs: ASK, BEG, IMPLORE, REQUEST

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "Please help me."                                                   │
│ REPORTED: She asked me to help her.                                           │
│           Me pidió que la ayudara.                                            │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Could you open the window?"                                        │
│ REPORTED: He asked me to open the window.                                     │
│           Me pidió que abriera la ventana.                                    │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Would you mind waiting?"                                           │
│ REPORTED: She asked me to wait.                                               │
│           Me pidió que esperara.                                              │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Can you pass the salt, please?"                                    │
│ REPORTED: He asked me to pass the salt.                                       │
│           Me pidió que le pasara la sal.                                      │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Please, please don't leave me!"                                    │
│ REPORTED: She begged him not to leave her.                                    │
│           Le suplicó que no la dejara.                                        │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

NEGATIVE REQUESTS:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "Please don't tell anyone."                                         │
│ REPORTED: He asked me not to tell anyone.                                     │
│           Me pidió que no se lo dijera a nadie.                               │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Could you please not smoke here?"                                  │
│ REPORTED: She asked him not to smoke there.                                   │
│           Le pidió que no fumara allí.                                        │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    REPORTED ADVICE
================================================================================

Common verbs: ADVISE, RECOMMEND, WARN, URGE, ENCOURAGE

ADVISE:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "You should see a doctor."                                          │
│ REPORTED: She advised me to see a doctor.                                     │
│           Me aconsejó que fuera al médico.                                    │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "You shouldn't eat so much sugar."                                  │
│ REPORTED: He advised me not to eat so much sugar.                             │
│           Me aconsejó que no comiera tanto azúcar.                            │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "If I were you, I'd take the job."                                  │
│ REPORTED: She advised me to take the job.                                     │
│           Me aconsejó que aceptara el trabajo.                                │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

RECOMMEND:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "You should try the fish."                                          │
│ REPORTED: She recommended trying the fish.                                    │
│           OR: She recommended that I try the fish.                            │
│           Recomendó probar el pescado.                                        │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WARN:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "Be careful! The floor is wet."                                     │
│ REPORTED: He warned me that the floor was wet.                                │
│           OR: He warned me to be careful.                                     │
│           Me advirtió de que el suelo estaba mojado.                          │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Don't go there alone."                                             │
│ REPORTED: She warned me not to go there alone.                                │
│           Me advirtió que no fuera allí solo.                                 │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Don't touch the wire or you'll get shocked!"                       │
│ REPORTED: He warned me not to touch the wire.                                 │
│           Me advirtió que no tocara el cable.                                 │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

ENCOURAGE:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "You can do it! Try again!"                                         │
│ REPORTED: She encouraged me to try again.                                     │
│           Me animó a intentarlo de nuevo.                                     │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "You should apply for the job."                                     │
│ REPORTED: He encouraged me to apply for the job.                              │
│           Me animó a solicitar el puesto.                                     │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

URGE:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "You really must leave now!"                                        │
│ REPORTED: She urged me to leave immediately.                                  │
│           Me instó a que me fuera inmediatamente.                             │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    OTHER REPORTING STRUCTURES
================================================================================

INVITE:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "Would you like to come to my party?"                               │
│ REPORTED: She invited me to come to her party.                                │
│           Me invitó a ir a su fiesta.                                         │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

REMIND:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "Don't forget to call your mother."                                 │
│ REPORTED: She reminded me to call my mother.                                  │
│           Me recordó que llamara a mi madre.                                  │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

OFFER:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "I'll help you with your bags."                                     │
│ REPORTED: He offered to help me with my bags.                                 │
│           Se ofreció a ayudarme con las bolsas.                               │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

PROMISE:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "I'll be there on time."                                            │
│ REPORTED: She promised to be there on time.                                   │
│           Prometió estar allí a tiempo.                                       │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

THREATEN:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "I'll call the police!"                                             │
│ REPORTED: He threatened to call the police.                                   │
│           Amenazó con llamar a la policía.                                    │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

REFUSE:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "No, I won't do it."                                                │
│ REPORTED: She refused to do it.                                               │
│           Se negó a hacerlo.                                                  │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

AGREE:
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "OK, I'll help you."                                                │
│ REPORTED: He agreed to help me.                                               │
│           Accedió a ayudarme.                                                 │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

================================================================================
                    SUGGEST (special case)
================================================================================

SUGGEST uses different structures (NOT infinitive with person):

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DIRECT:   "Let's go to the cinema."                                           │
│                                                                               │
│ REPORTED OPTIONS:                                                             │
│ • She suggested going to the cinema. (+ gerund)                               │
│ • She suggested (that) we go to the cinema. (+ subjunctive)                   │
│ • She suggested (that) we should go to the cinema. (+ should)                 │
│                                                                               │
│ ❌ She suggested me to go... (WRONG!)                                         │
│ ❌ She suggested us to go... (WRONG!)                                         │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ DIRECT:   "Why don't you take a break?"                                       │
│ REPORTED: He suggested taking a break.                                        │
│           OR: He suggested (that) I take a break.                             │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

                           QUICK SUMMARY
================================================================================

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│               REPORTED COMMANDS, REQUESTS, ADVICE                            │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ STRUCTURE: Verb + person + (not) + to + infinitive                           │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ COMMANDS:  tell, order, command, instruct                                    │
│            "Wait here." → She told me to wait there.                         │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ REQUESTS:  ask, beg, implore, request                                        │
│            "Please help me." → She asked me to help her.                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ ADVICE:    advise, warn, encourage, urge, recommend                          │
│            "You should rest." → He advised me to rest.                       │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ NEGATIVE:  + not + to + infinitive                                           │
│            "Don't go." → She told me not to go.                              │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ SUGGEST:   + gerund OR that + subject + (should) + verb                      │
│            "Let's eat." → She suggested eating.                              │
│            ❌ She suggested me to eat. (WRONG!)                              │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
